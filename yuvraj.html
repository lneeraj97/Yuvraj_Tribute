yuvraj singh

The man who won against all odds

Yuvraj Singh after making his highest score of 150 in ODIs against England

A Timeline of his life :

  • 1981 - Born on 12-12-1981 in Chandigarh,Punjab
  • 1995 - Played his first match for Punjab under-12 team against Jammu and Kashmir
  • 1996 - Got promoted to Punjab Under-19 team
  • 1997 - MAde his first class debut against Orissa in Ranji Trophy
  • 1999 - Scored a record 358 runs in an innings against Bihar Ranji Team
  • 1999 - Started playing for Indian Under-19 team
  • 2000 - Lead Indian Under-19 Team to a World Cup victory and bagged the "Player of the Tournament" award
  • 2000 - Made his International debut for India against Kenya
  • 2002 - Drew all the attention of International Media with his performance in the Natwest Series
  • 2003-04 - Had many ups and downs in his career
  • 2005 - Came back strong in the INdian Oil Cup
  • 2007 - Hit six sixes in an over against England
  • 2007 - Lead Indian T20 team to a historical World Cup victory
  • 2007-10 - Became one the most influential poster boys of Indain Cricket
  • 2011 - Gave an 'Out of the World' performance in 2011 World Cup and became the main reason behind India's WC victory and was the "Man of the Tournament"
  • 2011 - Diagnosed with Cancer
  • 2012 - Battled and defeated Cancer and came back to the Indian team
  • 2012 - Received the prestigious Arjuna Award
  • 2013 - Started YouWeCan Foundation
  • 2014 - Received the prestigious Padma Shri award
  • 2014 - Honored with FICCI Most Inspiring Sportsperson of the Year Award
  • 2015 - Got engaged to Hazel Keech
  • 2016 - Got married to Hazel Keech
  • 2017 - Still going strong in the International arena

    • "When one is young, aspiring to play for the country, doing well, any hindrance, like injury or being out of form, can be frustrating and a cause of annoyance or even anger. But once you have a close encounter with death, you realize the real value of life"

    Yuvraj Singh
